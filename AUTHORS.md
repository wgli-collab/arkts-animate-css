## arkts-animate-css Port

This HarmonyOS ArkTS port was produced through systematic migration of animate.css v4.1.1, adapting all 97 keyframe animations from CSS `@keyframes` to ArkTS `KeyframeState[]` functions while preserving the original animation behavior and naming conventions.

- **wgli-collab** — port, adaptation, and HarmonyOS-specific development

See also [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for how to contribute.
